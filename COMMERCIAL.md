## Do I need a commercial license?

You need a commercial license if you use PGVectorRAGIndexer in any of the following ways:

- in production environments
- as part of CI/CD or automated workflows
- for ongoing internal company use
- to support internal business operations
- as part of a paid, hosted, or client-facing service
- bundled or integrated into a commercial product

If you are a company evaluating PGVectorRAGIndexer to determine suitability,
short-term evaluation and testing are permitted under the Community License.

If you are unsure whether your use case qualifies as commercial, please reach out.

## What does a commercial license provide?

A commercial license grants:

- legal right to use PGVectorRAGIndexer for commercial purposes
- internal and production deployment rights
- CI/CD usage
- freedom from compliance uncertainty
- clear licensing terms suitable for procurement and legal review

Support, custom development, and consulting are optional and handled separately.

## Pricing approach

Commercial license pricing is based on:

- organization size

and scales with organizational size and usage scope.

Pricing is intended to be fair, transparent, and proportionate to value.
One-time, annual, and custom terms are available depending on use case.
